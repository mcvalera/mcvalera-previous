What is pair programming?

Pair programming is when two people work together as a team. They usually decide how they want to work together in terms of driving and navigating.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

Because one person doesn't know all the answers and it's good to work as a team. As a navigator, it helps to cement your learnings by teaching it to someone else, and as driver you can learn how other people think through things, and it teaches you patience. It prepares you for the workplace in that you learn how to work in a team.

Compare driver and navigator.

The driver is the person who is hands on with the programming, while the navigator acts as a guide.
